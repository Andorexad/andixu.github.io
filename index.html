About Research Project

I’m currently a master student in Stanford University majoring in Computer Science. I earned a BS in Computer Science at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, with minors in Mathematics and Japanese.

I’m interested in using AI to augment human capabilities and am motivated by the question, “How can we build systems that support humans while still ensuring their agency in this new AI era?”

In the past, I worked on HCI, accessibility and Human-AI interaction, fortunate to work under the supervision of Prof. Anhong Guo at Human-AI Lab.
